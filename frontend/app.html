What is AYUSH?

AYUSH is an acronym for the traditional and non-conventional systems of medicine practiced in India. It represents a holistic approach to health and wellness that has been refined over thousands of years.

üå±

Ayurveda

Origin: 5000+ years old, India

The "Science of Life" focuses on balancing the three doshas (Vata, Pitta, Kapha) through herbs, diet, and lifestyle modifications.

  • Herbal remedies and natural medicines
  • Personalized diet recommendations
  • Detoxification therapies (Panchakarma)
  • Holistic lifestyle guidance
üßò

Yoga

Origin: Ancient India, 3000+ BCE

A mind-body practice combining physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation for overall wellness.

  • Asanas (physical postures)
  • Pranayama (breathing exercises)
  • Meditation and mindfulness
  • Stress reduction and mental clarity
üåø

Unani

Origin: Greece, refined in Persia & India

Greco-Arabic medicine based on the theory of four humors, using natural healing methods and herbal formulations.

  • Herbal medicines and compounds
  • Dietary therapy (Ilaj-bil-Ghiza)
  • Regimental therapy
  • Preventive healthcare
üíä

Siddha

Origin: South India, Tamil Nadu

Ancient Tamil system using minerals, metals, and herbal compounds for treating chronic diseases.

  • Herbo-mineral preparations
  • Rejuvenation therapies
  • Detoxification methods
  • Chronic disease management

Homeopathy

Origin: Germany, 18th century

System based on "like cures like" principle, using highly diluted substances to stimulate the body's healing response.

  • Individualized treatment
  • Minimal side effects
  • Holistic approach
  • Safe for all ages
