About AYUSH

AYUSH represents five traditional systems of medicine that have been practiced in India for thousands of years. Our platform brings these ancient healing traditions into the modern age with AI-powered intelligence.

Ayurveda

The science of life, focusing on balance of body, mind, and spirit through natural remedies, diet, and lifestyle practices.

Yoga

Ancient practice combining physical postures, breathing techniques, and meditation for holistic wellness.

Unani

Greco-Arabic medicine system emphasizing balance of four humors and natural healing through herbs and minerals.

Siddha

Ancient Tamil system of medicine using minerals, metals, and herbs for treating chronic conditions.

Homeopathy

Holistic system based on "like cures like" principle, using highly diluted natural substances.
